# Piyal Gupta — Advisory

A static, glass-neumorphism consulting site for **Piyal Gupta** (Co-Founder &amp; CEO, Kneuralabs),
positioning his enterprise + AI-governance advisory services.

## What's here

- `index.html` — landing page
- `services.html` — the six advisory services
- `engagement.html` — the three engagement models
- `projects.html` — method and track record

No build step, no dependencies — served directly (e.g. via GitHub Pages).
`app.js` and `styles.css` are shared across all pages.

## Design

- **Style:** Ultra-minimalist glass neumorphism — a cool neutral canvas with
  paired light/dark shadows for extruded and inset surfaces, plus translucent
  glass panels.
- **Palette &amp; type:** logo-blue accent on a cool stone canvas, set in
  Cormorant Garamond / EB Garamond (display) and JetBrains Mono (labels/mono).
- **Dark mode:** toggle in the header, persisted to `localStorage`.
- **Motion:** hero headline typewriter effect and scroll-reveal, both
  respecting `prefers-reduced-motion`.

## Services presented

AI Governance &amp; Oversight · AI Risk &amp; Audit Assurance · Enterprise AI Adoption
Strategy · Bid Strategy &amp; Enterprise GTM · Cloud &amp; Delivery Transformation ·
AI-Augmented Creative &amp; Product — packaged into three engagement models
(AI Health Check, Build Your AI Rules, Your AI Advisor).
